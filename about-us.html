About us

findYourHEAVEN is a site that gives you a real picture of the life of an ex-pat with both positive and negative sides.

Through living in various countries, I have met different cultures and different people and experienced real life in some of them. Unfortunately, I also realized that many ex-pats are not objective and honest about their new life far from their homeland.

That is especially true for people from poorer backgrounds and often from developing countries where they didn’t have many opportunities, so they have this need to make themself worth in the eyes of their’s family, friends, and their’s community at home.

So, here I would do my best to give you a real picture of ex-pats life, and I would love it if you could share your experience of any country that you lived.
